BLEACH:BRAVE SOULS

Bleach: Brave Souls is a free-to-play gacha game developed by KLabGames and published by Konami. Set in the Bleach universe, it was released for Android and iOS in Japan in 2015 and worldwide early the following year. The game reported 40 million downloads by May 2019.
